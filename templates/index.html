Visitor Guidelines & Schedule

Operational Hours

  • Open Daily: 09:30 AM - 06:00 PM
  • Ticket counter closes at 05:30 PM
  • Planetarium Shows: Hourly from 11:00 AM
  • Closed only on Holi & Diwali

Visitor Policy

  • No food/beverages inside galleries
  • Arrive 15 mins before scheduled shows
  • Cloakroom available for large bags
  • Supervised exploration for children

  • Concessional rates for school groups
  • Official institutional letter required
  • Free entry for 1 escort per 10 students
